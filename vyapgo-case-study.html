The Problem

01 / 2026

India has 63 million MSMEs. Most owners are first-generation entrepreneurs with low digital literacy, juggling inventory, sales tracking, GST compliance, and customer management — all in their head or on paper. Existing software (Tally, Zoho, Vyapar) assumes literacy, a keyboard, and patience. None of them do.

  2. 02. Language barrier — tools are English-first; owners think in Hindi/regional
  3. 03. No time — shopkeepers can't stop selling to enter data
  4. 04. No identity — MSMEs have no digital presence of their own

The Solution

02 / 2026

VyapGO is a voice-first business OS for MSMEs. The user doesn't need to learn software. They just talk. The system does the rest — inventory, invoicing, compliance, analytics — all triggered through natural language in their preferred language.

Product Breakdown

03 / 2026
PRODUCT 01 — VyapYantra

"Your shop app. Built by your voice."

How It Works

  1. 01. Owner speaks their shop details, products, and categories in natural language
  2. 02. VyapYantra generates a branded mobile app — live, in real time, on screen
  3. 03. Owner reviews and downloads the app. No code. No forms. No agency.

What The App Does

Voice Inventory Management

"2 bottles of juice sold" → auto-deducted from stock

Daily Sales Dashboard

Visual summary of today's sales, revenue, units moved

Weekly Reports

Auto-generated PDF/summary every Sunday

AI Data Scientist

Analyses shop data + compares with online market data → gives recommendations (reduce price on X, sell Y in evenings, stock Z more)

GST Invoicing

Voice-triggered invoice generation, compliance-ready

Shop Identity

Every MSME gets a shareable digital storefront link

"60% reduction in data entry time. Owners spend more time selling, less time recording."

PRODUCT 02 — VyapSathi

"For shops where you can't hold a phone."

Kirana stores, vegetable vendors, dhaba owners — they're constantly using their hands. A phone-first product doesn't fit. VyapSathi is a physical AI device (think Amazon Echo) placed at the counter. The shopkeeper simply speaks — "Raju ne ek kg atta liya" — and it updates the app automatically.

  1. 01. Hands-free voice logging — syncs directly to VyapYantra app
  2. 02. Supports Hindi + regional languages
  3. 03. Works offline, syncs when connected
  4. 04. No screen required — audio confirmation of each action

Target user:Shops with high footfall where phones are impractical

PRODUCT 03 — VyapMandi

"A marketplace built on trust, not ads."

VyapMandi is VyapGO's quality-assured marketplace. Unlike Amazon or Meesho where any seller can list anything, VyapMandi lists only the top-performing product from each shop — verified by actual sales data inside VyapYantra. No paid placements. No fake reviews.

VyapGO is not a dashboard product. It's a behavioural shift — taking a shopkeeper who has never used software and turning them into someone who runs their business with data. The benchmark for success isn't DAU. It's whether the shop owner trusts the app enough to stop writing in their notebook.

Key Learnings

06 / 2026
001

Voice UX is harder than screen UX

Ambiguity in speech requires intent inference, not just transcription.

002

Trust before features

MSME owners adopt tools that feel like they were made for them. Generic SaaS triggers rejection. Vernacular onboarding changed everything.

003

The device is the product

VyapSathi shifted the product category from app to infrastructure. That changes the sales motion, pricing, and partnership strategy entirely.
